Agot.AI

Agot.ai is an Artificial Intelligence startup that provides computer vision restaurant management tools. I joined Agot.ai as its first designer and designed a cashierless checkout system and order accuracy tracking system to address consumer and employee painpoints and established a consistent brand identity.

ROLE:  Product Designer
Duation:   Summer 2020
METHODS:   User Research, UI/UX Design, Rapid Ideation, Prototyping, Animation
TOOLS:   Figma, Adobe Creative Suite, Framer

BACKGROUND

Agot.ai is a computer vision restaurant manager company that offers real-time solutions to increase service accuracy for fast food restaurants. Agot.ai currently provides a system to automate the checkout process through AI technology, but does not have an application that allows employees to increase service accuracy. As the first product designer, I was responsible for designing multiple customer and stakeholder centered products.

CHALLENGE

In light of the pandemic, contactless food ordering and payments have been embraced by customers to provide a safe and efficient experience.

The goal of my project is to explore ways to enhance the customer experience from checkout to order accuracy.

OUTCOME

Redesigned checkout system to allow customers to efficiently checkout via the restaurants tablet and designed a secure QR code checkout experience through personal mobile devices.

Designed an order accuracy system to streamline the order preparation process for employees and bring delight to customers beyond the store experience.

Startup signed an one million dollar contract with one of the largest restaurant chains in the country to implement the order accuracy system.

OUR SOLUTION

Order Accuracy

Agot's technology tracks each order from the point of sale to the preparation process. The kitchen display system provides a seamless experience for employees to be alerted of possible errors and how to correct the order.

Cashierless Checkout

Display computer vision generated order summary and details. Guide a smooth and efficient payment process for customers.

QR Code Payment

Secure contact-less payment on mobile devices to streamline the order payment process and address current challenges.

PROBLEM SPACE

An order accuracy system can improve the customer experience and aid in the reduction of food waste. Agot's technology effectively tracks an order throughout the preparation process but there is currently no employee facing experience to notify them of inaccurate orders.

How can we guarantee accurate orders?

The order accuracy system is a seamless experience for employees. The home screen is a live view of orders that are currently being prepared and keeps track of completed orders. When the system detects an incorrect orders, employees are notified to confirm the order they are preparing.

Clear instructions to fix inaccurate orders

After employees confirm the order they are preparing, they are presented with changes they need to make. We are currently user testing the following screens to determine the most effective way to convey the errors to employees. Employees receive clear instructions at this stage on the changes they must make to orders. Once the appropriate changes are made, the system automatically classifies the order as completed.

Seamless experience when no mistakes are detected

When prepared orders are correct, employees are notified as so and can proceed preparing other orders without interacting with the screen. Although this is an employee facing experience, it positively impacts the customer experience.

Customer Facing Problem

The cashier less checkout system Agot deployed at the time did not facilitate a smooth experience for both customers and employees. A contactless restaurant experience is necessary to ensure a safe experience for customers during the pandemic. The current checkout experience design did not optimize the order-to-fullfilmient process and lacked a consistent design language. The UI of the checkout lacked information architecture and contrast.

COMPETITIVE ANALYSIS

I conducted competitive analysis on existing restaurant cashier less checkout systems and I created user flows that reflected the user experience for each system. I then mapped the user flow of made-to-order restaurants and identified painpoints in the flow that Agot's technology addressed.

How can we facilitate an efficient and accurate checkout experience?

The redesigned checkout process alleviates the delayed checkout process in made-to-order restaurants. An accurate summary of each order is presented and customers are given the option to learn more details about their order. Customers can easily continue the checkout process on the POS system or can proceed with a contact-less method on their mobile devices.

Two-Step Payment Process

Customers are guided through the two-step payment process and can easily inquire for help from an employee. To a facilitate a contact-less experience, this system is currently focused on credit card purchases and reducing the number of clicks users need to make to complete their checkout.
